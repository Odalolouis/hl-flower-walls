1. **FAQ Schema with 8 Q&As** — These are designed to be directly extractable by AI systems as answer capsules

3. **Conversational phrasing** — Questions match how people ask AI assistants ("How much does it cost to rent a flower wall in Tampa Bay?")

3. **Yelp** — Claim and optimize your Tampa listing
4. **Thumbtack** — List your services
5. **Bark.com** — Register for event service leads
6. **Instagram** — Post consistently with location tags and service hashtags
7. **Facebook Business Page** — Fully optimized with all service details

**Create "Quotable" Content**

AI systems look for clear, authoritative statements they can cite. Add a blog to your site with posts like:
